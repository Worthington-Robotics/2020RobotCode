\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A \mbox{\hyperlink{classfrc_1_1lib_1_1motion_1_1MotionProfileGoal}{Motion\+Profile\+Goal}} defines a desired position and maximum velocity (at this position), along with the behavior that should be used to determine if we are at the goal and what to do if it is infeasible to reach the goal within the desired velocity bounds.
