\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A goal consists of a desired position and specified maximum velocity magnitude. But what should we do if we would reach the goal at a velocity greater than the maximum? This enum allows a user to specify a preference on behavior in this case. 

Example use-\/cases of each\+: 

O\+V\+E\+R\+S\+H\+O\+OT -\/ Generally used with a goal max\+\_\+abs\+\_\+vel of 0.\+0 to stop at the desired pos without violating any constraints. 

V\+I\+O\+L\+A\+T\+E\+\_\+\+M\+A\+X\+\_\+\+A\+C\+C\+EL -\/ If we absolutely do not want to pass the goal and are unwilling to violate the max\+\_\+abs\+\_\+vel (for example, there is an obstacle in front of us -\/ slam the brakes harder than we\textquotesingle{}d like in order to avoid hitting it). 

V\+I\+O\+L\+A\+T\+E\+\_\+\+M\+A\+X\+\_\+\+A\+B\+S\+\_\+\+V\+EL -\/ If the max velocity is just a general guideline and not a hard performance limit, it\textquotesingle{}s better to slightly exceed it to avoid skidding wheels.
