\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A controller for tracking a profile generated to attain a \mbox{\hyperlink{classfrc_1_1lib_1_1motion_1_1MotionProfileGoal}{Motion\+Profile\+Goal}}. The controller uses feedforward on acceleration, velocity, and position; proportional feedback on velocity and position; and integral feedback on position.
