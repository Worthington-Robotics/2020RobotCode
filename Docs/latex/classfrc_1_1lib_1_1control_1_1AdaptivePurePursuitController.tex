\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements an adaptive pure pursuit controller. See\+: \href{https://www.ri.cmu.edu/pub_files/pub1/kelly_alonzo_1994_4/kelly_alonzo_1994_4}{\texttt{ https\+://www.\+ri.\+cmu.\+edu/pub\+\_\+files/pub1/kelly\+\_\+alonzo\+\_\+1994\+\_\+4/kelly\+\_\+alonzo\+\_\+1994\+\_\+4}} .pdf

Basically, we find a spot on the path we\textquotesingle{}d like to follow and calculate the wheel speeds necessary to make us land on that spot. The target spot is a specified distance ahead of us, and we look further ahead the greater our tracking error.
