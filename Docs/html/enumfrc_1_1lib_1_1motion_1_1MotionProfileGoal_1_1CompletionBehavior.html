Detailed Description

A goal consists of a desired position and specified maximum velocity magnitude. But what should we do if we would reach the goal at a velocity greater than the maximum? This enum allows a user to specify a preference on behavior in this case.

Example use-cases of each:

OVERSHOOT - Generally used with a goal max_abs_vel of 0.0 to stop at the desired pos without violating any constraints.

VIOLATE_MAX_ACCEL - If we absolutely do not want to pass the goal and are unwilling to violate the max_abs_vel (for example, there is an obstacle in front of us - slam the brakes harder than we'd like in order to avoid hitting it).

VIOLATE_MAX_ABS_VEL - If the max velocity is just a general guideline and not a hard performance limit, it's better to slightly exceed it to avoid skidding wheels.
