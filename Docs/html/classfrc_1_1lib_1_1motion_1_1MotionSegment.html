Detailed Description

A MotionSegment is a movement from a start MotionState to an end MotionState with a constant acceleration.

Verifies that:

  1. All segments have a constant acceleration.
  1. All segments have monotonic position (sign of velocity doesn't change).
  1. The time, position, velocity, and acceleration of the profile are consistent.
